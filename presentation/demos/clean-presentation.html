What is the AGR MCP Server?

A bridge between AI assistants (like Claude) and genomics databases
  • Purpose: Programmatic access to Alliance of Genome Resources data
  • Protocol: Model Context Protocol (MCP) by Anthropic
  • Language: JavaScript/Node.js implementation
  • Target: Researchers, bioinformaticians, AI tools
